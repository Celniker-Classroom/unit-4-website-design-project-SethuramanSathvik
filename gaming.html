Sathvik's Hobbies - Gaming

I have always enjoyed gaming. Ever since I was a kid I would spend hours focusing on a nintendo switch or a playstation. However, as I progressed through the grades, I grew more and more disciplined on spending my time on gaming. Now, in 9th grade, I only game for two hours on the weekends. This is because during highschool, I want 100% of my focus to be on my grades and my other extracurricular activities. Don’t take me wrong, I still love gaming, perhaps even more than I used too, I just have the discipline to set the controller down when it’s time. Over the years of my gaming experience, 3 games have stood out to me the most: Helldivers 2, God of War, and Red Dead Redemption 2. The reason I enjoyed these games so much is because each one had something unique that set them apart from other games. They weren’t some overpriced, unfinished, rushed game made for a quick cash grab. They were made with patience and precision.
